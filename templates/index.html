About UTECH

Utech LLC was founded in 2008 to serve the needs of domestic automotive industry in the area of path planning and electronic stability control of ground vehicles.

The company played the leading role in interdisciplinary projects conducted by multinational teams of highly qualified experts in programing, structural dynamics and control.

Utech LLC is located in West Bloomfield, MI, USA.
